What is StuWork

StuWork is a community marketplace where students and adults of a school community can offer and accept mutually beneficial job opportunities

StuWork's Mission

Facilited through StuWork's user friendly website, adults act as employers and hire students for necessary jobs like yard work or tutoring while students earn a source of income, gain work skills, and take their first steps on the career ladder of success.
